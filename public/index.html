💞What is Heart Coherence?

Heart Coherence is a state of alignment between the heart, mind, emotions and body. Practicing heart coherence can help calm your nervous system and reduce stress. Learn more about it here!

✨Getting Started

1. Get connected to your HeartMath sensor and open up the emWave software.
2. In the emWave Interface, choose your challenge level to be Low, Medium, High, or Highest (we recommend starting with Low!) and press the round Play button. Now you're ready to start!👇
