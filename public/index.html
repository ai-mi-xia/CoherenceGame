What is Heart Coherence?

Heart Coherence is a state of alignment between the heart, mind, emotions and body. Practicing heart coherence can help calm your nervous system and reduce stress.

Getting Started

2. In the emWave Interface, choose your challenge level to be Low, Medium, High, or Highest (we recommend starting with Low!).
